Booking

If you are looking to play in a WSBF event, email events@wsbf.net. In your email, please include the following:

  • Group/Act name
  • Genre
  • Home town
  • Website
  • Link to a live performance video
  • Any other relevant information

You should receive a follow up email letting you know that your information has been received and you have been added to our database.

Note: Since WSBF is a part of Clemson University, booking a show is an intensive process. If a show is already announced, there will be no additions unless you are playing for free.

If you are looking to book WSBF for your own event, check out our Contact Us page.
