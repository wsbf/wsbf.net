How do I listen to a song that's in WSBF's archives or to-be-reviewed?

Listening to a song is pretty easy once you know how to access it.

  • First, go to the Music Library
  • Second, figure out where the album you want is. If you're reviewing it, it's probably under "To Be Reviewed". If it's a pretty old song, work your way through the other categories, starting from "Optional".
  • Use the search feature in the top right to search that category. Your search term will stay when you change to a different category.
  • To open the album, click on the "Album Code" in the left column. It'll probably look something like "K669".
  • From there, you can click the "Track No." to open an individual track. This will take you to an mp3 which you can listen to. Here's an example: "K669 Track 10".

    • Problem with the equipment?

    I can’t hear any sound coming from the speakers.

    • Are the speakers are on? There should be a white light on the front of both speakers.
    • Is the monitor volume set to a reasonable level?
    • Is the Prgm 1 button illuminated next to the monitor control?
    • Is there a source playing sound on the console?
      • Is the red ‘ON’ button illuminated on the channel playing sound?
      • Is the fader set to a reasonable level on the channel playing sound?
      • Is the Prgm 1 button illuminated at the top of the channel playing sound?
    • Is the ‘Cue’ button illuminated on any channels? If so, turn it off.
    • If you still can’t hear anything double check your source.
      • Is your phone or your laptop working?
      • Have you tired using a CD player?

    I can’t hear any sound coming from the headphones.

    • Can you hear sound from the speakers? If not, check the above list first.
    • Are the headphones plugged in properly?
    • Is the monitor volume set to a reasonable level?

    The delay unit does not show any remaining delay.

    • Press the green start button.
    • Wait for the delay time to increase.
    • If the screen reads ‘6100 BYPASSED’ press the BYPASS button.

    The CD player continues to play the next song on the CD.

    • Check the left side of CD player display.
    • It should read ‘SGL’.
    • f it reads ‘CNT’, press the ‘SINGLE/CONT.’ button.
