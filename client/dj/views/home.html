Welcome to the DJ Website!

If you're new to this website, here are the highlights:

  • If you're a new intern, you need to email the Chief Announcer (announcer@wsbf.net) to get intern status
  • You can check out and review albums under Music Library
  • You can edit your show information under User Profile
  • You can get MP3 archives of your shows under Archives
  • Can't make one of your shows? Submit a sub request under Show Sub Requests
  • Don't forget to log the good stuff you do for WSBF under Fishbowl Log
  • Want to know all the rules? Download the WSBF Handbook
  • Want to really know all the rules? Download the WSBF By-Laws

Still need more help? Check out the FAQ & Help for more specific things.
