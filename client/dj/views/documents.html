Music Policy

  • Songs must not use any of the 7 dirty words, and may not contain sexually explicit language
  • Once per hour, DJs can play two tracks from the same band, but they must be back-to-back.
  • No artist can be played on two consecutive shows.
  • Rotation Show: 75% Rotation - 25% from DJs personal collection or the WSBF Library
    • Music must be from artists that have not had a Billboard Hot 100 Hit in the last 10 years
    • Or had an a album go RIAA Gold (or greater) in the last 5 years

  • Specialty Show: No rotation music required
    • Music must be from artists that have not had a Billboard Hot 100 Hit in the last 5 years
    • Or have not had an album go RIAA Gold (or greater) in the last 2 years

Gold-Platinum Check: RIAA

What the hell is Fishbowl?

Fishbowl points are a reward system implemented to incentivize people to attend WSEvents and interact with the community at large. You can earn them through attending Committee meetings, completing sub requests, tabling, completing extra CD reviews, among other things. Basically, the greater the number of Fishbowl points you have, the earlier priority you get when selecting a show slot for the semester. Keep in mind that the amount of semesters you have had with WSBF will also factor into show slot priority.

Some reminders:

  • Use the Fishbowl Log option in the sidebar to log items.
  • Log any Commitee meetings, Events, or other activities you participated in.
  • Always put a description
  • Before you log the Other category, really consider if what you are about to put is really worth Fishbowl Points.
  • CD Reviews are logged automatically.
  • Filling a Sub Request is logged automatically.
